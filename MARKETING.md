### Body Copy (250 words)

Every quarter, thousands of teams go through the OKR ritual: set goals in January, check in once in March, score them in April, and wonder why they're in the same place as last year.

The problem isn't the framework. It's the implementation.

This skill gives you 4 structured AI prompts that build an OKR system with real teeth:

**Prompt 1 (Free):** Generate your company-level and department-level OKR tree in minutes. Includes an alignment matrix so every department KR traces back to a company objective — no orphaned goals.

**Prompt 2:** Cascade to individual contributors. Each IC gets their own OKRs, a confidence scoring rubric, and a dependency map so blocked work surfaces before it kills your quarter.

**Prompt 3:** Weekly check-in template with confidence scoring (1–5 scale), pacing alerts, and a manager rollup dashboard. 15 minutes per person per week. Mid-quarter reset protocol included.

**Prompt 4:** Full annual goal-setting workshop kit — facilitator script, timed agenda, breakout exercises, OKR quality filter checklist, and a post-workshop follow-up email. Run a 4-hour session that produces a real plan, not another slide deck.

Whether you're setting OKRs for the first time or fixing a system that isn't working, this skill covers the full lifecycle.

**Competitive Edge:** $29 once vs. $11–15/user/month for Lattice or 15Five (and those are just tracking platforms — they don't write your OKRs).
